MCYCD Mission

Supporting and Encouraging Cultural and Societal Movements and Promoting the UAE National Identity through effective coordination with our partners and through distinction in our services and human resources using creative and developed means.

MCYCD Vision

Leadership in Cultural and Societal Development for a Prosperous UAE.

MCYCD Objectives

  • First Objective: Supporting cultural activities and elevating the level of UAE cultural awareness.
  • Second Objective: Preserving the Cultural Identity and promoting its components.
  • Third Objective: Upgrading the artistic movement and preserving the intangible UAE heritage.
  • Fourth Objective: Promoting the integration and coordination with both public and private sectors and building effective partnerships.
  • Fifth Objective: Development of the human resources and the institutional performance according to applied international practices.

Public Libraries Objectives

  • To meet the needs of the information resources to the different classes of the community.
  • To support researchers, schools and universities students those are wishing to develop their information skills.
  • To encourage reading attitude to children, young people and adults.
  • To encourage information knowledge skills and high-level reading.
  • To encourage in participating on the social life and mass participation.
  • To motivate to take advantage of the leisure times by spending in libraries and participation in their programs.
  • To provide the modern developed information technology in order to facilitate and access the libraries Collections.

Public Libraries Services

The Ministry provides through the public libraries a variety bunch of libraries services to meet the need of community of libraries members as per their different age's classes and their educational and cultural backgrounds within machinery and channels safe providing required fast and efficient services. Libraries services provided by the libraries include the following:

    Main service

  • Membership service.
  • Circulation service.
  • Shared Library.
  • Advisory Visits service.

    • Supplementary services

  • Reference services
  • Guided tours
  • Membership and circulation services.
  • Self check.
  • On-line Catalog.
  • Book reserve service.
  • Internet service.
  • Current awareness service.
  • Electronic library.
  • Sharing Library.
  • Documents coping service.
  • Printing and typing service.
  • Scanner service.
  • Request of library materials.
  • Special services for people with special needs.
